Key Insights

Strong Demand

93% of respondents expressed interest in a smart sock with AI voice reminders, with 32% being "very" or "extremely" interested.

Family Alerts #1

Real-time alerts to family members (69%) ranked as the most valued feature, followed by emergency notifications (58%) and continuous health monitoring (58%).

Accuracy Matters

Health monitoring accuracy (59%) is the top concern, followed by comfort (37%) and cost (25%) — signaling that reliability must come before pricing.

Price Sensitivity

77% of respondents expect a starter kit under $99 (670 RMB), with nearly half (50%) preferring under $50 (340 RMB).
